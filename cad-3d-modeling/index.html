3D CAD modeling services

Parametric 3D CAD modeling for industrial design and fabrication

Creation, cleanup and improvement of CAD 3D models for components, assemblies and technical communication, including support for files from SolidWorks, Inventor, AutoCAD, Plant 3D, Rhinoceros and standard CAD exchange formats.

Models prepared for review, manufacturing and future edits

3D CAD modeling is useful when a company needs clean geometry, organized assemblies or CAD/CAM-compatible data without rebuilding everything from scratch.

The goal is to reduce rework and improve communication between design, workshop, supplier and technical decision makers.

Deliverables

  • Parametric 3D component models
  • Assembly structure and file organization
  • Neutral CAD export formats

Problems solved

  • Models that are hard to edit
  • Missing files or unclear references
  • Geometry that does not support fabrication review
