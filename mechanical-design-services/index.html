Practical design for parts, assemblies and industrial equipment

The work considers structural and functional constraints before geometry becomes a finished CAD model. This includes fabrication feasibility, access for assembly, maintenance needs and how the design will be explained to the production team.

It is suitable for mechanical design services, product design for manufacturing, industrial CAD modeling, machine components, agricultural or industrial equipment and existing designs that need review before production.
