Documentation that supports real fabrication

Technical drawings organize the information needed for quoting, fabrication, assembly and supplier communication. Dimensions, tolerances when applicable, materials, quantities and assembly intent are treated as practical production information.

The service can start from existing CAD models, sketches, measurements or partial documentation that needs to become a clear manufacturing package.

Deliverables

  • Manufacturing drawings and assembly views
  • Dimensions, notes and tolerances when applicable
  • BOM support and organized documentation packages

Problems solved

  • Unclear workshop or supplier communication
  • Missing information before quotation or fabrication
  • CAD models without usable technical documentation
